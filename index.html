    WHAT WE OFFER

    Our Activities

    At Tymex child care services, we offer a range of activities, including arts and crafts, music and movement, sensory play, story time, and science and discovery. Outdoor activities like games and nature walks are also included. Children Spa and Health treatment are also a part of what we do. Dramatic play and quiet time activities like reading and puzzles are also part of the mix. These activities cater to different ages and interests, promoting learning and fun. The goal is to engage children in meaningful experiences that foster growth and development.

    ABOUT US

    We are a nurturing and safe child care services provider dedicated to offering a stimulating and engaging environment for children to grow and thrive. Our team of experienced and passionate caregivers strive to provide personalized attention and care to each child, fostering their physical, emotional, and cognitive development.

    OUR MISSION

    At Tymex, we aim to provide a warm, welcoming, and inclusive space for children to explore, learn, and play. We believe in partnering with parents and guardians to build a strong foundation for lifelong learning and happiness.

    OUR VALUES

    • Safety and Security: We prioritize the well-being and safety of every child in our care.
    • Quality Care: We deliver exceptional care and attention to each child's unique needs.
    • Inclusivity and Diversity: We celebrate and embrace the diversity of our children and families.
    • Learning and Development: We provide engaging activities and experiences that promote cognitive, social, and emotional growth.
